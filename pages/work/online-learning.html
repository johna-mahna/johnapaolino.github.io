Process

Collaboration across a range of clients, topics, and campuses has been an exciting challenge. One of the best techniques I have found to address this has been the use of asynchronous ideation surveys. I will send out a Google Form containing sketches and questions, which allows me to aggregate feedback and move designs forward with clear qualitative and quantitative feedback.

Low-fi sketches & animations

Communicating ideas around student progress and engagement is really difficult to do staying static. This project has forced me to produce lots of low fidelity animations to help share, test and validate ideas.
