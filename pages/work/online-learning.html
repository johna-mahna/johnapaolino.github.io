Studies in Online Learning

Most of the content at General Assembly is delivered offline. It is really important that the innovative ideas executed in the classroom manifest themselves in the online learning system. One of my primary tasks has been better define interactions and interfaces for content delivery across all topics.

Process

Collaboration across a range of clients, topics, and campuses has been an exciting challenge. One of the best techniques I have found to address this has been the use of asynchronous ideation surveys. I send out idea sketches and questions through Google Forms. This allows me to aggregate feedback and move designs forward with clear qualitative and quantitative feedback.
